## How It Works
1. **Connect your wallet** (MetaMask required)  
2. **Enter election**  
3. **Vote for your favorite candidate**  
4. **After the vote has processed, click 'Get Winner'** 
5. **Wait for the random winner to be selected**  
6. **See if your candidate won**
